Services & pricing

Setup, maintenance, repair, and blade care

Prices below are starting estimates — final cost depends on your specific mower model and the size or layout of your lawn. Contact us for an exact quote.

01 — At your home

Initial Setup & Programming

We visit your property, install or position the charging station, walk the lawn with you, and program the mower's boundaries, schedule, and zones.

Standard Setup

Starting at $129

Charging station placement, boundary wire or beacon setup, and basic mowing schedule for lawns under 1/4 acre.

Large or Complex Lawn

Starting at $179

For larger lawns, multiple zones, narrow passages, slopes, or properties needing custom no-go area mapping.

App & Schedule Setup Only

Starting at $59

Already have the hardware installed? We'll configure the mowing schedule, zones, and app settings to match your routine.

02 — Maintenance & repair

Diagnostics and repair, on-site or in our workshop

Some issues we can fix at your home. Others — especially anything needing specialized tools or parts — are best handled back at our workshop. We'll always tell you which option makes more sense before doing any work.

In-Home Service Call

Starting at $69 + parts

For issues we can usually resolve on-site: sensor problems, software resets, docking/charging issues, wheel and motor checks, and minor adjustments.

Workshop Diagnostics & Repair

Starting at $49 diagnostic + parts/labor

Drop your mower off at our office for a full diagnostic. We'll identify the issue, give you a repair estimate, and only proceed once you approve it.

03 — Blade care

Blade replacement & sharpening

Dull or damaged blades make your mower work harder and leave a rougher cut. This is one of the quickest and most affordable services we offer.

Blade Sharpening

Starting at $25

We remove, sharpen, and rebalance your existing blades. Usually done same-day at our workshop.

Blade Replacement

Starting at $20 + cost of blades

Worn or damaged blades replaced with new ones suited to your mower's make and model.

Not sure what your mower needs?

Tell us what's going on and we'll point you toward the right service — or let you know if it sounds like something simple you can try first.
