About us

Local, hands-on robotic mower service

We started GreenLine because robotic mowers are great — until something needs adjusting, and most people don't have the time or tools to deal with it themselves. That's where we come in.

Our approach

We treat your lawn like it's the only one we're working on

Every yard is different — different shapes, slopes, obstacles, and grass types. When we set up a mower, we walk the property with you, map out the boundaries and no-go zones, and program the mower so it covers your lawn the way you'd want it to, not just the default settings.

When something goes wrong, we don't guess. We run full diagnostics to find the actual issue, whether that's a software glitch, a sensor problem, a worn part, or just a dull blade. If it's something small, we'll often fix it on the spot. If it needs more time, we'll take it back to the workshop and keep you updated.

Why people choose us

  • On-site setup tailored to your lawn's actual layout
  • Honest diagnostics — we tell you what's actually wrong
  • Flexible options: drop it off, or we come to you
  • Quick turnaround on common fixes like blades and sensors
  • Friendly, no-pressure advice on keeping your mower running

Have a robotic mower that needs some attention?

Get in touch and we'll figure out the best way to help — whether that's a visit to your home or a quick drop-off.
