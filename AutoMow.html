What we do

Setup, maintenance, and repair — all in one place

Whether your mower is brand new or just needs a tune-up, we handle it on-site or in our workshop, whichever gets you back to a perfect lawn faster.

Initial Setup & Mapping

We come to your home, install the charging station, map your lawn's boundaries, and program the mower to handle your yard's specific layout.

Maintenance & Repair

Diagnostics, software updates, sensor issues, and mechanical repairs. Drop your mower off at our office or, for bigger issues, we'll come to you.

Blade Service

Quick, affordable blade replacement and sharpening to keep your cuts clean and your mower running efficiently.
